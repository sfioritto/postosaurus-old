Stay on task.

Sometimes you just need a to-do list.

Managing your task list with Postosaurus is as easy as sending an email. Or you can manage your tasks using a web application through your browser; it's up to you.

Postosaurus automatically generates email reminders to help you remember when you need to get started on something. and tells you when anyone creates, updates or completes a task.
