Your project runs on email. Postosaurus makes email better.

Postosaurus uses a private email list to track your project's communications, files, links and tasks.

It's easy to use and takes minutes to get started.

  • Email

    Use one email address to communicate with your entire group.

    Every email is stored in a searchable archive.

  • Files

    Postosaurus manages your project's documents.

    To add a file to your project, attach it to an email and send it to your group. See every version of every file emailed to your project in one place.

  • Links

    All of your project's urls in one place.

    Email a link to your group and Postosaurus will store it where you can find it later. No more fruitless searching for "that link you sent."

  • Tasks (Coming soon.)

    Email yourself or someone else a task to do later.

    Postosaurus builds task lists from emails automatically. View all of your project's tasks from one place without losing the convenience of using email.

Do I need to create a new email account?

No. When you create a Postosaurus group you get a private email list for your group. You send email to that address from your own email address and it goes to everyone in your group.

Do I need to download and install software?

No. Postosaurus is entirely web-based. In fact, you don't even have to use the web site as most activities can be accomplished using plain old email.

If I cancel, what happens to my data?

Even if you cancel your plan you still have access to all of your data, you just can't add new links, files or send emails to your group.

You begin your project using a traditional project management tool, but in the end everyone in your group skips using the tool and sends emails. Why fight it? If you know how to use email you know how to use Postosaurus. Your group members never even need to sign in to the web site. To start using Postosaurus, simply send an email to the group.
