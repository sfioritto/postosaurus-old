Why Postosaurus?

You've tried Microsoft Project, spreadsheets, sharing Google documents, and every other project management application on the planet. But everyone in your group always goes back to email.

Why fight it? Postosaurus makes email better.

What is Postosaurus?

Postosaurus is a lightweight project management application built around email. If you know how to use email, you know how to use Postosaurus.

How does it work?

It works like an email group. Everyone in your group uses one email address to talk with everyone else in the group. Plus you get tasks, document management and Postosaurus will even remember the links you send to each other.
