  • How does the 15 day free trial work?

    If you choose to cancel during your 15-day free trial, you won't be charged for anything. After your free trial you will be charged a prorated fee for the remainder of the month and at your plan's normal rate for each month thereafter. We do not issue refunds once your 15 day trial has ended and we do not prorate partial months.

  • What if I need another plan?

    We will be releasing more plans soon so you can upgrade and downgrade to whatever works the best for you. If you need a different plan right away, contact us here, and we'll set you up.

  • Do I need to sign a long-term contract?

    No. Postosaurus plans are month-to-month. You can cancel, upgrade or downgrade at any time.
